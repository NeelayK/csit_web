Chemical Society of IISER Thiruvananthapuram (CSIT)
We are a group of chemistry enthusiasts who seek to learn and share knowledge about anything relating to the chemical world with the wider student fraternity, and also have fun in the process. We organize several events towards this end, including but not limited to competitions, talks, workshops and seminars. We also have an active social media presence, where we share informative and fun content.
